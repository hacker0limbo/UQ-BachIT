    Fleur

    Fleur is the primary designer for this project.

    She is currently completing a Dual degree with a Bachelor of Information Technology and a Bachelor of Business Management at the University of Queensland.

    In the past, Fleur has done some design work for her high school Moreton Bay College. She designed the 2018 school Diary Cover, 2017 Semi Formal Invitations, 2018 Locker Designs for her house and the Senior shorts logo. She also developed promotional work for various school and house events such as Founders Day, the Alison Green Charity Event, etc.

    Fleur’s interests include photography, public speaking, trampolining, creative writing and reading.
